Ethiopia Smart Police Station — Platform Overview

🎬 What This Platform Does

  • Crime Reporting: Citizens can securely file crime reports 24/7 without visiting a physical station. Your Fayda ID verifies your identity.
  • Evidence Upload: Upload documents, photos, and videos directly from your device. Files are encrypted and tagged to your case.
  • Officer Communication: Chat and video call directly with assigned officers. Real-time, encrypted, and private.
  • Case Tracking: Monitor the status of your filed report from Pending → Investigating → Resolved in real time.
  • Secure Database: Every interaction is stored in an encrypted local database on the police station's secure servers — no third-party cloud.
  • Admin Oversight: Police administrators can view all cases, user profiles, uploaded documents, and communication logs through a secure admin panel.
  • Fayda + TIN Verification: Identity is verified via Ethiopia's Fayda national ID and Tax Identification Number to prevent fraudulent reports.
