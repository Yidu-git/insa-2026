# Introduction
---
**Open Source Intelligence** or OSINT the <mark style="background: #CACFD9A6;">process of collecting, analyzing, and synthesizing publicly available information to produce actionable insights</mark>. The opensource in OSINT refers to publicly available and legally accessible data.

Its mainly used by Law Enforcement, Journalists and Cyber Security teams.

# How does OSINT work?
---
**OSINT** Relies on collecting information from publicly available data. By combining and correlating data from multiple datasets, Analysts can form a picture about the target.

## Main data source

#### OSINT EXAMPLE
The most common form of OSINT is an online background check on a persons name and profile. This process is usually done by checking a persons **social media accounts** and posts. When done properly, the data collected can reveal a persons character, connections, backstory and even the persons entire personality.
